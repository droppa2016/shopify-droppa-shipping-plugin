Thank you for donwloading the Droppa Shipping App.

In order to activate the Droppa Shipping App, you will have to provide us with your API and Service keys generated from the Droppa Group through our support team. Once those keys are enterd, you can navigate to your online store and start making purchases, you will notice a courier method listed Droppa Shipping with an amount to it, you'll have to select it and continue with the order until payment in order to create a booking with the Droppa Group company.

Please click on the Settings link to capture your API keys.
